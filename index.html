About the Candidate

Meet Karba

Karba Eckley has spent their career turning environmental expertise into real-world results — and now they're bringing that work home to Montalvo.

As a Program Manager at The Energy Coalition, Karba leads programs that help public agencies across California cut energy costs, reduce emissions, and build resilience. They co-authored the City of West Hollywood's Equitable Building Performance Standard and spent years at Patagonia developing corporate climate strategy and quantifying Scope 3 emissions. They are a Certified Energy Manager with a Master's degree in Environmental Science and Management from UC Santa Barbara.

But Karba isn't just an expert — they're a neighbor. As Secretary of the Montalvo Area Community Council, they've shown up to city hall meetings, pushed for climate accountability, and advocated for a Ventura that works for everyone who lives here. They know this district, and they're ready to fight for it.

Priorities

What Karba Will Fight For

District 6 needs a councilmember with the technical depth and community commitment to turn policy into action.

🌱

Climate Resilience

Ventura faces real risks from wildfires, drought, and sea-level rise. Karba will push the city to fully fund and implement its Climate Action and Resilience Plan — not just endorse it.

⚡

Clean Energy & Lower Bills

With deep expertise in energy efficiency programs, Karba will connect Ventura residents and businesses to funding, incentives, and upgrades that reduce costs and carbon emissions.

🏘️

A Livable Montalvo

District 6 deserves investment — in safe streets, green infrastructure, and a community that's ready for the future. Karba will make sure Montalvo's voice is heard at City Hall.
